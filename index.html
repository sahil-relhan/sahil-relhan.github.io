About Techsurge & Mridang

Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University is a premier institute in the field of technology. The Institute is established by Maharaja Agrasen Technical Education Society promoted by group of well known Industrialists, Businessmen, Professionals and Philanthropists with an aim to promote quality education in the field of Technology.


The knowledge, skill and acumen instilled in the students has been instrumenal in creating prospective leaders for tomorrow and making them stand above the hoi polloi.

Techsurge & Mridang 2018 is a three days Techno-Cultural fest Which include Technical events under the banner of "Techsurge", Cultural events under the banner of "Mridang and various Non-Tech and Sports events."

Table tennis, also known as ping pong, is a sport in which two or four players hit a lightweight ball back and forth across a table using small bats. The game takes place on a hard table divided by a net. Except for the initial serve, the rules are generally as follows: players must allow a ball played toward them to bounce one time on their side of the table, and must return it so that it bounces on the opposite side at least once. A point is scored when a player fails to return the ball within the rules. Play is fast and demands quick reactions. Spinning the ball alters its trajectory and limits an opponent's options, giving the hitter a great advantage.
